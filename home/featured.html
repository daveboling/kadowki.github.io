Live DemoGithub Repository

Capstone Project #1

A no-nonense learning utility that encourages good form and gives the ability for others to challenge those with similar knowledge. As a life-long learner, it is my belief that forming good study habits early will lead to a much deeper understanding of any subject. Knipit is an attempt to not only build upon already existing tools, but to also create challenges for students while encrouaging good form at the same time. Knipit provides a chance for users to really prove what they know and not only will that user prove it to someone else, but they will also prove it to themselves.

Gravity Flip

After a trip through the Milky Way, your crew is ready to return to Earth. However, a terrible storm of asteroids and debris block your path. During your trip, you notice that the inertia and gravitational pull are constantly reversing. Navigate your way through this anomaly so that you can tell Earth of your findings!

How to play:

  1. Tap your finger on the screen where you want your ship to go.
  2. Don't go off the screen or hit an piece of debris! If you do, your ship will be destroyed.
  3. Have fun!
